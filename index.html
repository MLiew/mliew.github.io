Projects

Here includes some of my research projects and also personal projects.

Satellite Collision Risk Analysis based on Data Mining

Analyze the collision risk of orbiting spacecraft according to the density of spacecraft distribution in space using clustering and classification methods to filter out the spacecraft with higher collision risk with the target satellite.

  • Learn More

Parameter Updating for Digital Satellite Model via Data Mining Approach

Created an ontology-based knowledge graph that contains the relationships between satellite state variables and parameters. By utilizing graph reasoning and clustering algorithms, it helps to update the selected variables of the digital satellite model.

  • Learn More

Capsule Closet App

Capsule Closet is an Android application developed to keep track the items we have in the closet. By having a good grasp of the closet situation, users are able to purchase clothes rationally, and hence reduce their desire for trendy clothes.
